What Is CheeseToken?

CheeseToken is like a big wheel of digital cheese—1 quadrillion pieces to start with! It’s built on Shibarium, a speedy blockchain tied to the Shiba Inu family. You can trade it, hold it, or play our special "catch" game to snag some from others. Every catch makes the total amount of CHEESE shrink a bit, so it gets rarer over time.

How the Game Works

The fun part of CheeseToken is the "catch" game. You can try to grab some CHEESE from others, but there are rules to keep it fair and exciting. Here’s how it all works:

Who’s Who in the Game

There are three types of players, based on another token called NoneToken:

  • Dogs: The big players in the Shiba Inu world.
  • Cats: Those who’ve paid some BONE to join the fun.
  • Rats: Everyone else just holding CHEESE.

Fun Fact: Your role can change if you step up in the Shiba Inu ecosystem!

How to Catch CHEESE

Want to catch some CHEESE? Here’s the deal:

  • Step 1: Send exactly 1 CHEESE to someone’s wallet.
  • What Happens: You take 1% of their CHEESE stash!
  • You Get: 0.7% of their stash goes to you.
  • The Twist: 0.3% gets burned—poof, gone forever!
  • Catch Limit: You can only catch once every 20 hours.

Example: If they have 1,000 CHEESE, you send 1, take 10 (1%), keep 7 (0.7%), and 3 (0.3%) disappear. Cool, right?

Game Rules to Know

  • Who Can Catch: Only Dogs and Cats can play the catching game.
  • Who Can’t Be Caught: Dogs and special "protected" wallets (on a whitelist) are safe.
  • Cat Rule: Cats can only catch Rats, not other Cats.
  • No Rest for Targets: If you’re a target, you can be caught over and over—no breaks!
  • Minimum Catch: Targets need at least 100 tiny CHEESE bits (0.0000000000000001 CHEESE) to be worth catching.

Why the Burn?

Every time someone catches, 0.3% of the snatched CHEESE gets burned. This means the total amount of CHEESE out there shrinks a little each time. Over time, CHEESE could become harder to find—and maybe more valuable!

Keeping It Safe

We’ve got a special list called the "whitelist" to protect certain wallets—like pools or important players—from being caught. Only the token creator can add or remove wallets from this list. They can also pause or start the game anytime, but once they give up control, it’s game on forever!

Catchers wait 20 hours, but targets? No rest—watch your cheese!

Tracking the Fun

Every catch gets logged—like a trophy wall! You can see who caught what, how much they got, and when it happened. It’s all stored on Shibarium, so the history is there for everyone to check out.

Shibarium is super fast—every move takes just 5 seconds! Plus, it uses BONE, the Shiba Inu family’s favorite coin, to keep things running. It’s the perfect playground for CheeseToken’s quick catches and playful vibes.
